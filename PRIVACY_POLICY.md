Privacy Policy for Status Pro
Last Updated: February 12, 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how Status Pro (Anjeer Soft) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.
1. Information Collection and Use
Status Pro is designed to help you save and manage status media. We prioritize your privacy and do not collect personal identification information.
•
Media Files: The app requires access to your device's storage to read, display, and save status images and videos. These files remain on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.
•
Permissions: We request READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (or the equivalent "All Files Access" on newer Android versions) solely to provide the core functionality of viewing and saving media.
2. Third-Party Services
We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
•
Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data such as your advertising ID to personalize ads. Google's Privacy Policy.
•
In-App Purchases: Payments are processed via the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not see or store your credit card details.
3. Data Retention
The app does not have a backend server. Any "Saved" statuses or "Favorites" are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this local data may be removed depending on your OS settings.
4. Children’s Privacy
Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
